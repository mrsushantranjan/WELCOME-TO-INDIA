Culture of Telangana

Telangana has a rich cultural heritage with unique art forms like Bathukamma, Perini dance, and distinctive cuisine including Hyderabadi Biryani. The state celebrates festivals with great enthusiasm, blending Hindu and Muslim traditions in a unique way.

Wedding Style

Telangana weddings are vibrant celebrations with traditional rituals, colorful attire, and elaborate feasts featuring authentic Telangana cuisine. The ceremonies often include Pellikuthuru (bride's reception), Mangala Snanam (holy bath), and Kashi Yatra rituals.
