Discover the Charm of West Bengal

From the Himalayas to the Sundarbans, Bengal is a blend of culture, nature, and history.

Top 12 Tourist Places in West Bengal

Victoria Memorial

Iconic white marble monument in Kolkata.

Sundarbans

Largest mangrove forest and home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.

Darjeeling

Famous for tea gardens, toy train, and Himalayan views.

Howrah Bridge

Engineering marvel connecting Kolkata and Howrah.

Kalighat Kali Temple

Sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Kali.

Digha Beach

Popular seaside destination for relaxation.

Shantiniketan

Founded by Rabindranath Tagore, a hub of art and culture.

Mukutmanipur

Picturesque dam surrounded by hills and forests.

Bishnupur

Known for terracotta temples and Baluchari sarees.

Belur Math

Spiritual headquarters of the Ramakrishna Mission.

Kurseong

Quiet hill station famous for orchids and tea.

Cooch Behar Palace

Beautiful royal palace inspired by European architecture.

Culture of West Bengal

West Bengal is famous for Durga Puja celebrations, Bengali literature, Rabindra Sangeet, traditional art, and sweets like Rosogolla and Sandesh.

Wedding Style

Bengali weddings feature the bride in a red Benarasi saree, groom in dhoti-kurta with a 'Topor', and rituals like 'Shubho Drishti' and 'Saat Paak'.
