Culture of Bihar

Bihar is famous for Madhubani paintings, folk dances, Bhojpuri music, and festivals like Chhath Puja. It has deep roots in Buddhism and Jainism, making it a unique cultural and spiritual hub.

Wedding Style

Traditional Bihari weddings feature vibrant sarees, dhotis, folk songs, and rituals accompanied by dholak beats and joyous cultural performances.
