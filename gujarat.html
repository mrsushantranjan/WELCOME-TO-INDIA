Top 12 Tourist Places in Gujarat

Gir National Park

Home to the majestic Asiatic lions, a wildlife lover’s paradise.

Somnath Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva.

Dwarka

Ancient city associated with Lord Krishna.

Rann of Kutch

Salt desert famous for Rann Utsav festival.

Saputara

Only hill station of Gujarat with cool climate.

Champaner-Pavagadh

UNESCO World Heritage site with forts and temples.

Ahmedabad Heritage City

India's first UNESCO World Heritage City.

Modhera Sun Temple

Stunning temple dedicated to the Sun God.

Diu

Coastal town with beaches and Portuguese forts.

Junagadh

Historical city with forts and caves.

Mandvi Beach

Serene beach perfect for relaxation.

Palitana Temples

World’s largest temple complex.

Culture of Gujarat

Gujarat has a rich cultural heritage with colorful festivals like Navratri, traditional folk dances such as Garba and Dandiya, and unique crafts like Patola sarees and Bandhani.

Wedding Style

Gujarat weddings are vibrant, with rituals like Garba nights, colorful attire, and grand feasts featuring Gujarati thalis.
