Discover the Backwaters of Kerala

From Alleppey to Munnar, enjoy nature, culture, and Ayurveda

Top 12 Tourist Spots in Kerala

Alleppey

Famous for houseboats and serene backwaters.

Munnar

Tea plantations and misty hills.

Kovalam

Golden sands and beach vibes.

Thekkady

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and spice gardens.

Wayanad

Forests, waterfalls, and wildlife.

Kochi

Historic port city with fishing nets and spice markets.

Varkala

Cliffside beach and yoga retreats.

Bekal Fort

Sea-facing fort with panoramic views.

Thrissur

Cultural capital famous for Thrissur Pooram.

Kumarakom

Backwaters and bird sanctuary.

Ponmudi

Hill station with winding roads and misty peaks.

Silent Valley

Pristine rainforest with rich biodiversity.

Culture of Kerala

Kerala is famous for Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, temple festivals, and Ayurveda traditions, blending Hindu, Muslim, and Christian heritage.

Wedding Style

Weddings in Kerala are elegant, featuring gold jewelry, Kasavu sarees, and simple temple or church rituals.

Major Crops

  • Coconut
  • Spices (Cardamom, Pepper)
  • Tea & Coffee
  • Rubber
  • Paddy (Rice)
