Culture of Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known for its royal heritage, colorful festivals like Desert Festival and Teej, folk dances like Ghoomar and Kalbeliya, and intricate handicrafts including block printing, blue pottery, and miniature paintings.

Wedding Style

Rajasthani weddings are royal and grand, featuring traditional attire, elephant or horse processions, and elaborate feasts with Dal-Baati-Churma and Ghevar.
