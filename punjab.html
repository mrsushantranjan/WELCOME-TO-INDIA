Culture of Punjab

Punjab is known for its vibrant Bhangra and Giddha dances, colorful festivals like Lohri and Baisakhi, and its warm hospitality. Traditional crafts like Phulkari embroidery are world-famous.

Wedding Style

Punjabi weddings are lively affairs with dhol beats, colorful turbans, phulkari dupattas, and sumptuous Punjabi cuisine served in style.
