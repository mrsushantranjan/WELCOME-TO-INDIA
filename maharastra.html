Top 12 Tourist Places in Maharashtra

Gateway of India

Iconic monument in Mumbai facing the Arabian Sea.

Ajanta Caves

Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with stunning paintings.

Ellora Caves

UNESCO site with Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain monuments.

Shirdi

Sacred shrine of Sai Baba, attracting millions of devotees.

Lonavala

Popular hill station famous for its greenery and chikki.

Mahabaleshwar

Hill station known for strawberries and viewpoints.

Tadoba National Park

Wildlife sanctuary known for tigers and biodiversity.

Sindhudurg Fort

Sea fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Elephanta Caves

Island caves with rock-cut sculptures of Lord Shiva.

Alibaug Beach

Coastal town with serene beaches and forts.

CSMT Mumbai

UNESCO-listed railway station with Gothic architecture.

Pandharpur

Important pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Vitthal.

Culture of Maharashtra

Maharashtra is famous for Lavani dance, Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations, and Warli tribal art. It blends historic Maratha traditions with modern urban culture.

Wedding Style

Traditional weddings feature Paithani sarees, dhoti-kurta, pheta (turban), and rituals set to the music of shehnai and dhol-tasha.
