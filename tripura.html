Top 12 Tourist Places in Tripura

Neermahal Palace

Magnificent water palace located in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake.

Ujjayanta Palace

Former royal palace of Tripura, now a state museum.

Tripura Sundari Temple

One of the 51 Shakti Peethas, dedicated to Goddess Kali.

Unakoti

Ancient Shaivite pilgrimage site with massive rock carvings.

Jampui Hills

Beautiful hill range offering panoramic views and orange gardens.

Bhuvaneswari Temple

Ancient temple on the banks of River Gomati.

Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary

Home to diverse flora and fauna including spectacled monkeys.

Dumboor Lake

Picturesque lake with 48 islands, shaped like a drum.

Mata Tripureshwari Temple

Important Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Tripura Sundari.

Gunabati Group of Temples

Historic temple complex showcasing Tripura's architectural heritage.

Rowa Wildlife Sanctuary

Small but significant wildlife sanctuary with diverse species.

Agartala

Capital city with rich cultural heritage and modern amenities.

Culture of Tripura

Tripura has a rich cultural heritage with diverse tribal communities, traditional dances like Hojagiri and Garia, colorful festivals like Kharchi Puja and Ker Puja, and unique handicrafts including bamboo and cane products.

Wedding Style

Tripura weddings are vibrant celebrations that blend Hindu rituals with tribal traditions, featuring colorful traditional attire, folk music, and elaborate feasts with local delicacies.
