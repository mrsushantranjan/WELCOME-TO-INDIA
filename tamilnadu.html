Culture of Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is known for Bharatanatyam dance, Carnatic music, temple festivals, and Pongal celebrations. Its culture is deeply rooted in Tamil literature and traditions that date back over two thousand years. The state is famous for its classical arts, traditional silk sarees, and elaborate temple architecture.

Wedding Style

Weddings feature Kanchipuram silk sarees, veshti, gold jewelry, jasmine flowers, and traditional nadaswaram music. Tamil weddings are known for their vibrant rituals, including the Nicchyathartham (engagement), Mangala Snaanam (holy bath), and Kanyadaanam (giving away the bride).

Major Crops

  • Rice - The staple food and primary crop
  • Sugarcane - Widely cultivated for sugar production
  • Cotton - Important for textile industry
  • Bananas - Tamil Nadu is a leading producer
  • Coconut - Grown extensively in coastal regions
  • Tea and Coffee - Plantations in hill stations
